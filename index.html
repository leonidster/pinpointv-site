Frequently asked questions

Everything about deciding what to watch with PinpoinTV.

What is PinpoinTV?

PinpoinTV is a free Android TV and Google TV app that helps you decide what to watch. You tell it your mood, genre, and content type, and its AI recommends one movie or show — limited to the streaming services you already pay for, so every suggestion is something you can actually watch right now.

How does PinpoinTV decide what to watch?

You pick your mood (like light and funny, tense, or cozy), then a genre and whether you want a movie or a series. PinpoinTV's AI uses that, plus the streaming services you have and your country, to pinpoint one tailored recommendation with a poster and details instead of a wall of thumbnails.

Is PinpoinTV free?

Yes. PinpoinTV is completely free on Google Play for Android TV and Google TV, and no account is required to use it.

Do I need to sign in?

No. PinpoinTV works fully without an account. Signing in with your Google account is optional and only backs up and syncs your History and Favorites across devices. You can sign out any time, and you can request full account deletion — see our Privacy Policy and account deletion page.

Which devices does PinpoinTV work on?

PinpoinTV is built for Android TV and Google TV, with big cards and remote-friendly D-pad navigation designed to be readable from the couch.

Does PinpoinTV work with my streaming services like Netflix, Prime Video, and Disney+?

Yes. You tell PinpoinTV which streaming services you subscribe to, and it only recommends titles available on those services, so it never suggests something locked behind a subscription you don't have.

Can PinpoinTV recommend a movie or show based on my mood?

Yes, mood is the starting point. Instead of browsing a broad genre, you choose how you feel tonight and PinpoinTV's AI narrows thousands of options down to one confident pick.

How is PinpoinTV different from JustWatch or Reelgood?

Apps like JustWatch and Reelgood are searchable catalogs. PinpoinTV is a decision tool: it's mood-first, made for the TV remote, and gives you one AI-chosen recommendation from your own subscriptions instead of a long list to scroll.
